# LensDay — Account & Data Deletion

To request deletion of your LensDay account and all associated 
data, or to request deletion of specific data only, please contact 
us by email:

**Email:** privacy@lensday.app  
**Subject line:** Account Deletion Request  
or  
**Subject line:** Data Deletion Request

Please include the email address associated with your LensDay 
account. We will process your request within 30 days in accordance 
with GDPR Article 17.

**What gets deleted:**  
- Your account and login credentials  
- All check-in photos and videos  
- All coaching responses  
- Your profile and preference data  

**What may be retained:**  
- Anonymised, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you  
- Transaction records required for legal or tax compliance  

For questions, contact privacy@lensday.app
