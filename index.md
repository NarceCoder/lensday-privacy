# Privacy Policy — LensDay

**Last updated:** 25 March 2026

---

## 1. Who We Are

LensDay ("we", "our", or "us") is an Android mobile application providing AI-powered personal coaching services. We are based in the European Union and operate in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

**Email:** privacy@lensday.app

---

## 2. The Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data when you use LensDay:

### 2.1 Data You Provide Directly
- **Your name** — provided during onboarding, used to personalise your coaching experience.
- **Photos and videos (selfies)** — captured in-app during daily check-ins and submitted for AI analysis. This is the core input of the coaching service.
- **Text messages** — short messages you optionally write alongside your check-in.
- **Profile information** — responses to optional onboarding and enrichment questions, including approximate age range, lifestyle habits, preferences, and goals. All such questions include a "Prefer not to say" option. Answers marked as such are never stored or processed.

### 2.2 Data Collected Automatically
- **Device information** — device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, collected for technical support and compatibility purposes.
- **Usage analytics** — anonymised data about how you interact with the app (e.g. screens visited, features used, session duration). This data does not identify you personally.

### 2.3 Data We Do Not Collect
We do not collect:
- Precise geolocation data
- Financial or payment card information (subscription billing is handled entirely by Google Play)
- Biometric identifiers beyond the selfie images you voluntarily submit
- Data from your device's contacts, calendar, or other apps

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes, each grounded in a lawful basis under GDPR Article 6:

| Purpose | Data Used | Lawful Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Delivering AI coaching responses | Selfies, videos, text messages, profile data | Performance of contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Personalising coaching over time | Profile information, historical check-ins | Performance of contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Sending daily reminder notifications | Device identifiers | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Improving app performance and stability | Device info, usage analytics | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Complying with legal obligations | Any data as required | Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) |

We do not use your data for advertising purposes. We do not sell your data to third parties.

---

## 4. Third-Party AI Providers

To deliver coaching responses, your check-in data (selfie or video, optional text message, and relevant profile context) is transmitted to third-party AI providers for processing. These providers analyse the content and return a generated coaching response.

We may use one or more of the following providers, or comparable services, depending on the current version of the app:

- OpenAI (GPT series models) — Privacy Policy: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
- Google (Gemini models) — Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
- Anthropic (Claude models) — Privacy Policy: anthropic.com/privacy

**Important:** Your data is transmitted to these providers solely to generate your coaching response. We do not permit these providers to use your data to train their models, and we select providers whose data processing agreements include this restriction. Data transmitted is not linked to your identity by the provider — it is sent as an anonymous request.

We will update this section if our AI provider changes. The current provider in use is disclosed in the app's Settings screen.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

Your check-in history, coaching responses, and profile data are stored securely in our database infrastructure.

- **Check-in data** (selfies, videos, text, responses) is retained for as long as your account is active, plus 30 days following account deletion to allow for recovery requests.
- **Profile data** is retained for the duration of your account.
- **Usage analytics** are retained in anonymised, aggregated form for up to 24 months.
- **Device information** is retained for up to 12 months.

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. These include:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher)
- Encryption of data at rest
- Access controls limiting who within our organisation can access personal data
- Regular security reviews

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security concern related to LensDay, please contact us immediately at the address in Section 1.

## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject in the European Union, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access** — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification** — you may request correction of inaccurate data.
- **Right to erasure** ("right to be forgotten") — you may request deletion of your personal data.
- **Right to restriction** — you may request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
- **Right to data portability** — you may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Right to object** — you may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address in Section 1. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
